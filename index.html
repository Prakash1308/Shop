    About Us

    Sri Lakshmi Pavani Gold House is a family-owned and run business,
    selling the finest quality jewellery. We are situated in Chirala, Andhra Pradesh.

    All of Our jewellery is handmade using the finest materials including pure gold,
    semi-precious stones, and silverware.

    We don't just want to sell you jewellery, We want to build a relationship with you.

    We are an BIS-certified jewellery merchant with 25 years of experience in
    manufacturing hand-made jewellery.

    Every piece of jewellery carved out of pure gold in 22-carat.
